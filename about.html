about us

COMMUNITY SKILLS DEVELOPMENT CENTRE KATSINA (COSDEC).

The center was established by the year 1974 since the Kaduna state so as provide necessary skills to our teaming youth that enable them to become self-employed, self-reliance and self-sufficient.

We are providing yearly enrolment of (male and female) student in differnt trades.

The trades that are currently offered in the center are:

  • Building and Mansory
  • Carpentry and Joinery
  • Garment Making
  • Hospitality Management
  • Office Managemnt (ICT)
  • Fabrication and Welding
  • Plumbing and pipe Fittings
  • Electrical Installation
  • Cosometology
